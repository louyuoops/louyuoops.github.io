一元二次方程求解

$$ \begin{array}{l}a x^{2}+b x+c=0 . \\ a x^{2}+b x=-c \\ 4 a \cdot a x^{2}+4 a b x=-4 a c \\ (2 a x)^{2}+2 \cdot(2 a x \cdot b)=-4 a c \\ (2 a x)^{2}+2 \cdot(2 a x \cdot b)+b^{2}=b^{2}-4 a c \\ (2 a x+b)^{2}=b^{2}-4 a c \\ 2 a x+b= \pm \sqrt{b^{2}-4 a c} \\ x=\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^{2}-4 a c}}{2 a}\end{array} $$

三角恒等式

  • 毕达哥拉斯定理

$$ cos^2(x) + sin^2(x) = 1 \\
1+tan^2(x)=sec^2(x) \\ cot^2(x)+1=csc^2(x) \\ $$

  • 恒等式

$$ cos(\pi/2-x) = sin(x)\\ sin(\pi/2-x) = cos(x)\\ cot(\pi/2-x)=tan(x)\\ tan(\pi/2-x)=cot(x)\\ sec(\pi/2-x)=csc(x)\\ csc(\pi/2-x)=sec(x)\\ sin(A+B)=sin(A)*cos(B)+cos(A)*sin(B)\\ cos(A+B)=cos(A)*cos(B)-sin(A)*sin(B)\\ sin(A-B)=sin(A)*cos(B)-cos(A)*sin(B)\\ cos(A-B)=cos(A)*cos(B)+sin(A)*sin(B)\\ sin(2x) = 2sin(x)cos(x)\\ cos(2x)=cos^2(x)-sin^2(x)\\ $$
